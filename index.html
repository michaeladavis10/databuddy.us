Our Approach: From Complexity to Clarity

At DataBuddy, we believe data consulting should be clear and focused. Our approach is designed to cut through complexity and provide you with a straightforward path to achieving your business goals. Consider these analogies:

The "Knee Pain" Analogy

Imagine you want to run a marathon, but you have knee pain. The doctor diagnoses the pain, the physical therapist gives you exercises, and the insurance company dictates the treatment. Each specialist solves their piece of the puzzle, but no one is focused on your actual goal: running the marathon. You're left to navigate this complex system alone, acting as your own advocate.

This is exactly how businesses experience data problems. You're sold a dashboard tool, a cloud platform, and a consulting workshop, but no one is accountable for the business result. DataBuddy acts as your performance coach and advocate. We start with your ultimate goal—the marathon—and build the single, cohesive strategy that gets you across the finish line.

The "Financial Overwhelm" Analogy

Think about managing your business's finances. You're an expert in your industry, but not a tax accountant or corporate lawyer. You know you need to optimize for growth, but you're wary of advisors with hidden fees or their own agendas. You don't know who to trust or what questions to ask.

This is how many owners feel about their data. The landscape of vendors and tools is confusing and expensive. DataBuddy acts as your trusted advisor, but for your data. We speak your language—business outcomes—and demystify the technical world, ensuring you never overspend on technology you don't need and that every decision aligns with your long-term goals.

Proven Impact, Real Results

We are relentlessly outcome-oriented. Here’s how we’ve helped other businesses turn data into a competitive advantage.

Data Strategy Reboot for a National Governing Body of Sport

Challenge: A national governing body of sport was plagued by poor data quality, overwhelming their internal team. We found the root cause was a deep disconnect between their operational practices and vendor capabilities.

Solution: As part-time consultants, we diagnosed the core issues and recommended a full strategic reboot. We then delivered the initial technical specifications to launch a new, properly aligned data ecosystem.

Result: The organization shifted from treating symptoms to tackling the root cause, equipped with a strategic roadmap to build a data foundation that would support their mission for years to come.

Rescuing a University AI Research Project

Challenge: When a key developer left a university AI/ML research project, they left behind critical, non-working code with zero documentation, putting the entire project at risk.

Solution: We performed a technical rescue: reverse-engineering the complex code, restoring functionality, and creating comprehensive documentation to empower the remaining team.

Result: The project was saved from a standstill. The team was no longer dependent on a single person and could continue their important research independently and with confidence.

Guiding R&D Investment for a Medical Device Innovator

Challenge: A non-technical team was struggling to analyze disjointed data to vet new products. The real goal was to make "go/no-go" decisions before committing to multi-million dollar R&D investments.

Solution: We aggregated 9 disparate data sources into a unified dashboard with clear recommendations. We then trained their team, taking them from struggling with Excel to confidently running Python scripts in under 6 months.

Result: The company saved millions by identifying non-viable projects months ahead of schedule, and their team was up-leveled with lasting, independent data skills.

Strategic Data Partner for a Private Equity Firm

Challenge: A PE firm acquiring HVAC companies had no in-house technical advocate, leaving them reliant on potentially biased guidance from vendors and subsidiary staff.

Solution: We served as their dedicated technical partner, translating complex data migration options into clear business outcomes. We then executed the hands-on migrations to their new, standardized platform.

Result: The PE firm was de-risked from vendor lock-in and could confidently accelerate its acquisition strategy. Once their needs were met, we advocated for our own dismissal, ensuring maximum client value.

Building a Data Curriculum for an EdTech Startup

Challenge: An online startup needed to develop a data analysis curriculum but lacked the expertise to create practical content and train their instructors to teach it effectively.

Solution: Leveraging our experience as an Adjunct Lecturer at two universities, we designed a key-skill-focused curriculum and then trained their instructors on how to effectively lead their own cohorts.

Result: The startup launched a successful new program with a fully-trained instructional team. We provided the curriculum, trained the trainers, and moved on, empowering them to scale independently.

Building a Profit Engine for a Master Craftsman

Challenge: A home remodel contractor with superior craftsmanship was consistently losing money by underbidding jobs and mis-estimating timelines because he dreaded the administrative overhead.

Solution: We built a simple yet powerful solution in Google Sheets that connected directly to his Home Depot account, requiring only a single entry per day to accurately track all project expenses and time.

Result: We immediately identified thousands of dollars in unbilled materials for return, and built a solid dataset that now empowers him to bid on future jobs with confidence and profitability.
